✦ The Curriculum

12 months. Real progress.

Every student follows a structured month-by-month curriculum. You always know exactly what you're learning and where you're headed.

1
Hello Piano!
Hand posture, finger numbers, first 3 notes
2
First Melody
Mary Had a Little Lamb, finger numbers 1–5
3
Right Hand Sings
Ode to Joy, smooth finger movement
4
Left Hand Wakes Up
Left hand intro, simple bass notes
5
Hands Together!
Twinkle Twinkle, coordination basics
6
My First Full Song
Complete song hands together, legato & staccato
7
Feeling the Music
Dynamics, student's chosen song
8
Chords Are Fun

9
Reading Music

10
Speed & Flow

Express Yourself

12
Recital Ready! 🎉
